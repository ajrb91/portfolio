# ⚡ RLCCONV

**Description:**  
Program developed for HP graphing calculators (HP49g, HP48gII, HP49g+, HP50g) to calculate the **electromagnetic parameters of transmission lines** — resistance, inductance, and capacitance — using **User-RPL** language.

**Author:** Amílcar José Rodríguez Barrios  
**Year:** 2012  
**Technology:** User-RPL (HP Calculator Programming)  
**Application Area:** Electrical Engineering – Power Systems  
**Purpose:** Academic tool for students and professionals to simplify and automate calculations of single-phase and three-phase transmission line parameters.  
**License:** Free use and distribution, modification not allowed.
